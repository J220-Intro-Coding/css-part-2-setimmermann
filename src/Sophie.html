Gourmet Pizza dinner in 30 min

Ingredients

Pizza toppings

  • 1kg diced tomatoes
  • 2 Monteli Organic Pizza crusts (any substitue is possible)
  • bundle of fresh Ruccola
  • 2 paprika
  • 1 onion
  • 1 mozarella cheese
  • 1 pack of organic shredded Mozarella cheese
  • selection of Calabrese Salami (can be substituted with similar / vegetarian option)
  • optional: black olives
  • Herbs de Provence
 

Salad

  • 1 bundle of lettuce
  • 2 avocados
  • 500 g cherry tomatoes
  • 3-4 carrots
  • For salad sauce: olive oil, Italian vinegar, pepper, salt, mustard, honey

Directions

 

Step 1 Open the tin of diced tomatoes and boil sauce in a pan

 

Step 2 Start pealing and cutting the onion, paprika, mozarella

 

Step 3 Preheat oven to 450 degrees F

 

Step 4 Add tomato sauce evenly to Pizza crust

 

Step 5 Add vegetables and salami to the crust

 

Step 6 Put Pizza in the oven and bake for 10 minutes

 

Step 7 Wash lettuce and put in a bowl

 

Step 8 Cut avocados, carrots and cherry tomatoes and add to bowl with lettuce

 

Step 9 Mix 3 spoons of olive oil, vinegar, salt, pepper, teaspoon of mustard and honey

 

Voila! Ready is your gourmet Pizza dinner!
